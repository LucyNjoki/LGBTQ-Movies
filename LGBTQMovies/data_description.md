The movies_red dataset contains information about LGBTQ+ Movies.

- title: Title of the movie in English.
- original_title: Original title of the movie, in its original language.
- original_language: Language in which the movie was originally produced.
- genre: List of genres associated with the movie.
- overview: Short synopsis or description of the movie.
- release_date: Official release date of the movie (format: YYYY-MM-DD).
- popularity: Popularity score.
- vote_average: Average rating of the movie given by users.
- vote_count: Total number of user votes received.
- adult: Indicates whether the movie is marked as adult content (TRUE or FALSE).
